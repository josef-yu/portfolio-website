The site is live! After years stuck in construction due to my busy schedule, here we are, the very first blog written.

I plan to share my ramblings here to improve my writing skills. I will be covering many topics from personal musings to anything about tech. Don't worry, it's not going to be heavy unless it's necessary. I hope the world will go easy on me.

The site is built on Astro, and plan to build further tooling on content creation of the site. Of course, comments and reactions can be created on my blogs which is powered by [giscus](https://giscus.app).

Anyway, with AI being part of the development workflow nowadays, writing and communication have become more important in the software development industry. So I'll just take it one blog at a time outside of work and one documentation at a time inside of work.
